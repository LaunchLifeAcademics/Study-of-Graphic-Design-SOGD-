Introduction to SAMA and Niches

In this module, you will be introduced to our fictional client, SAMA, a lifestyle brand inspired by Moroccan and Spanish heritage. You will explore SAMA in greater detail later as part of your work on design research later on in this module. For now, your task is to think about the design niches involved in the brand’s work.

The Client and Your Question

SAMA was co-founded by two second-generation sisters from Moroccan and Spanish backgrounds. The company is preparing to launch a limited collection of candles, incense, and home textiles inspired by their heritage, including mosaic patterns, desert florals, terracotta tones, and Andalusian tilework. The collection will be sold online and promoted mainly through Instagram and boutique pop-up events. SAMA also plans to use an AR app, idescu.eu, to allow customers to virtually preview selected textiles in their own homes.
